ABOUT US

From the Autopreneur, Alumnus from MIT Manipal, The organizer of the G Karting had designed and built first Go Karting Racing Car of his District, Founded Motorsports Company, Designed and built Go Karting Circuit for a Resort, Achieved Youngest Entrepreneur award from a reputed Autonomous Institution. A true petrolhead who wants the niche of technology to reach every budding Engineer.

G Karting is a venture of passionate and enthusiastic engineers who endeavor towards inculcating within the student fraternity a sense of real time application of their engineering knowledge and give them first class exposure regarding industry requirements and practical experience. Striding hard to introduce motorsports at the university level.

G Karting especially designed to quench the thirst for motorsports in engineering students. This is a true platform for the igniting petrolhead to learn, design, fabricate and race their own Go-Karting vehicle.
